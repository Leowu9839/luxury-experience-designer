## A Soul-Searching Question

> He spends $1,000 on a Michelin three-star meal. You get 80g of wagyu (two slices), 30g of foie gras (one bite).
> You spend $30. You get 150g of wagyu (2×), 200g of foie gras (6×).
> 
> **He paid 30× more. He ate less meat than you.**

This isn't sour grapes. This is **luxury democracy**.

---

## What This Is

**LuxeHack** is a systematic luxury-experience-for-everyone AI Skill.

It doesn't do luxury shopping guides. It doesn't do penny-pinching tips. What it does: **Uses knowledge and creativity to help you live like the ultra-rich on a budget — transforming "I can't afford that" into "I can experience that, and I will."**

### Core Methodology: Six Dimensions of Substitution

| Dimension | Core Logic | Example |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| 🌍 **Origin Substitution** | Same quality, different origin = vastly different price | French foie gras → Anhui Huoqiu foie gras |
| 🏠 **Scene Substitution** | Core experience unchanged, scene swap = cost plummets | Private island → Public hidden island + booked B&B |
| ⏰ **Time Substitution** | Off-peak/off-season = time-for-price trade | Peak private jet → Off-peak first class + VIP channel |
| 📦 **Scale Substitution** | From "limited tasting" to "unlimited enjoyment" | He tastes one sip of wine → You buy a whole case |
| 🔧 **Tech Substitution** | Use modern tech to replicate scarce experiences | Real space → VR spacewalk + white noise + gaming chair |
| 🎭 **Narrative Substitution** | Boost experience through ritual/story packaging | Host a "private tasting" at home with friends |

### The "Go All Out" Philosophy

The rich experience **"limited tasting"** — pay a fortune for one bite, one hour, two nights.

Your experience is **"unlimited enjoyment"** — at equivalent utility, you outmatch them on quantity and freedom with spare change.

**Goal: Transform budget constraints into active, rational choices.**

## Real Example: $70 vs. Michelin Three-Star

**User input**: Experience Michelin three-star, budget $70, core desire = taste thrill + ritual + social currency

**LuxeHack output**:

| Dimension | Rich Person's Cost | Your Cost | Gap |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------|-----|
| 💰 Money | $600-1,200/person | $30 | 20-40× |
| ⏱️ Time | Book 2-3 months ahead + 4-hour meal | Shop 10 min + cook 30 min | ~100× |
| 🎲 Risk | Can't return if bad, restaurant picks guests | Zero risk | Infinite |
| 😤 Hassle | Formal dress, concierge coordination, social pressure | Zero hassle | Infinite |

**Core Plan**:
- Order Australian M9 wagyu (150g ~$12) + Huoqiu foie gras (200g ~$8) + Yunnan truffle sauce ($4)
- Pan-sear at home, white plate + sauce drizzle + rosemary garnish, candles lit
- Invite 1-2 friends, personally serve and explain ingredient trivia
- Social post: "Tonight's chef's table: wagyu medium-rare, foie gras with apple. No 3-month wait. No suit required."

**Soul punch**:
> He wants to go all out? Not on the menu. Can't add it. Chef's narrative can't be interrupted.
> You want to go all out? Place order. Unbox. Fire up the pan. Sizzle to perfection — **who's stopping you?**

## Quick Start

Tell LuxeHack three things:

```markdown
[Scene] What do you want to experience?
→ Example: Michelin dining, private island, top-tier spa, limited whiskey

[Budget] How much do you have? (Can be $0)
→ Example: $70, $300, $0 pure brainstorming

[Core Desire] What kind of thrill do you want most?
→ Example: Taste thrill, ritual, social currency, exploratory curiosity
```

**Output Structure (Six-Step Loop)**:

```
Step 0: 🎩 Advisor Opening — Output on first invocation
Step 1: 🎯 Align with Real Rich Experience — Show you how the rich actually do it (demystified)
Step 2: 🔧 Experience Deconstruction & Substitution — Six dimensions, with utility ratings
Step 3: 📋 Tiered Plan Output — Core plan / Bonus additions / Approximation plan
Step 4: ✅ Final Accounting & Value Comparison — Thrill coverage, money saved
Step 5: 💸 People's Victory · Precise Calculation — Quantity/Time/Freedom/Combo crushing
Step 6: 💡 Advisor Closing Statement — You've already lived the thrill within budget
```

## Core Philosophy

Ultimate joy has never been defined by physically arriving somewhere or obtaining some label.

Often what you envy isn't the rich person's experience — it's **the filter in your imagination**. Once you align the granularity, you'll find: **It's not that far. You can do it too.**

You're not settling for less. You're shattering the "only the rich deserve this" mental barrier, using knowledge and creativity to genuinely indulge within your budget.

**Knowledge and creativity are the currency of the new era.**
